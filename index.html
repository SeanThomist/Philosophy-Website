philosopher | educator

King's College London  ·  London, UK

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Philosophy at King's College London. My research is motivated by a simple question: how do we understand one another? From this question flows my work on empathy, emotions, and conversation.

Beyond the academy, I am involved in public philosophy education in prisons, schools, and art galleries.

My PhD is generously funded by the Ramsay Centre for Western Civilisation.
