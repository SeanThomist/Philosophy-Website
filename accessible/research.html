Scholarship

Research

My work is motivated by a simple question: how do we understand one another? From this question flows my work on empathy, emotions, and conversation.

Areas of Specialisation

  • Philosophy of Mind and Phenomenology
  • History of Phenomenology
  • Social Epistemology

Areas of Competence

  • Epistemology
  • Ethics (especially sentimentalism)
  • Feminist Philosophy
